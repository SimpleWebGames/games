Single player games

Blackjack

Classic game of Blackjack. Blackjack pays 3 to 2. Dealer stands on soft 17. No insurance is offered. You can split only once. Doubling down after splitting is not allowed but you can draw more than one card after splitting aces. When splitting, you always start by playing your first deck.

Two players games

Cycles

Arcade style

Low FPS

Tow players battle each other. If you hit your trail, the trail of the other player or the wall, you lose. Player 1 uses the arrow keys to change direction and Player 2 the W, A, S and D keys.

Play

Ping

Arcade style

Score points by sending the ball past your opponent's paddle. Player 1 uses the W and S keys to move the paddle up dans down and Player 2 the UP and DOWN arrow keys.

Play

Sicario

Arcade style

Violent

Win by killing your opponent. Player 1 uses the W, A, S and D keys to move and the E key to shoot. Player 2 uses the I, J, K and L keys to move and the U key to shoot. Both players can lock the direction they are facing by pressing the C and N keys, respectively.
